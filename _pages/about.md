I am a researcher at the **Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB)**, 
working at the intersection of **Machine Learning** and its real-world 
applications in **Sports Analytics** and **Healthcare**.

My research focuses on developing and applying advanced machine learning 
models to extract meaningful insights from complex datasets — helping 
organizations make smarter, data-driven decisions in performance optimization 
and clinical outcomes.

## Research Interests

- **Sports Analytics** — Performance prediction, player evaluation, injury 
  risk modeling, and game strategy optimization using ML techniques
- **Healthcare Analytics** — Disease prediction, patient outcome modeling, 
  clinical decision support systems, and medical data analysis
- **Machine Learning** — Deep learning, predictive modeling, time-series 
  analysis, and statistical learning methods

## What I Do

In **sports**, I apply machine learning to understand athlete performance 
patterns, predict outcomes, and provide actionable insights to coaches and 
analysts. My work bridges the gap between raw sports data and strategic 
decision-making.

In **healthcare**, I work on building intelligent models that can assist 
clinicians in early diagnosis, risk stratification, and treatment planning — 
making healthcare more precise and proactive.

## Background

I am based in Bengaluru, India, and am affiliated with the Indian Institute 
of Management Bangalore. I am passionate about using data science to solve 
real-world problems that have a direct impact on human performance and 
well-being.
